Risotto

Description

Risotto is a northern Italian rice dish cooked with broth until it reaches a creamy consistency. The broth can be derived from meat, fish, or vegetables. Many types of risotto contain butter, onion, white wine, and parmesan cheese. It is one of the most common ways of cooking rice in Italy. Risotto is widely known for the uncanny taste and exquisite taste

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups chicken broth
  • 1 1/2 cups beef broth
  • 1/2 cup white wine
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 1/2 cup chopped celery
  • 1/2 cup chopped carrot
  • 1/2 cup chopped green bell pepper
  • 1/2 cup chopped red bell pepper
  • 1/2 cup chopped mushrooms
  • 1/2 cup chopped zucchini
  • 1/2 cup chopped yellow squash
  • 1/2 cup chopped tomato
  • 1/2 cup chopped spinach
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh basil
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh thyme
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh oregano
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh chives
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh tarragon
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh rosemary
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh sage
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh marjoram
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh dill
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh mint
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh lemon balm
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh lemon thyme
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh lemon verbena
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh lavender
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh chervil
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh savory
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh bay leaf
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh tarragon
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh rosemary

Steps

  1. In a large saucepan, heat chicken and beef broth over medium heat. Keep warm.
  2. In a large skillet, heat oil over medium heat. Add onion, celery, carrot, and bell peppers. Cook, stirring occasionally, until vegetables are tender, about 10 minutes.
  3. Add mushrooms, zucchini, and yellow squash. Cook, stirring occasionally, until vegetables are tender, about 5 minutes.
  4. Stir in tomato, spinach, basil, thyme, oregano, parsley, cilantro, chives, tarragon, rosemary, sage, marjoram, dill, mint, lemon balm, lemon thyme, lemon verbena, lavender, chervil, savory, and bay leaf. Cook, stirring occasionally, until vegetables are tender, about 5 minutes.
  5. Stir in rice. Cook, stirring occasionally, until rice is lightly toasted, about 5 minutes.
  6. Stir in wine. Cook, stirring occasionally, until wine is absorbed, about 5 minutes.
  7. Stir in 1 cup warm broth. Cook, stirring occasionally, until broth is absorbed, about 5 minutes.
  8. Continue adding broth, 1 cup at a time, stirring occasionally, until rice is tender and creamy, about 20 minutes.
  9. Remove from heat. Stir in cheese. Serve immediately.
